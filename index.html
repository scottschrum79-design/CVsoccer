Volunteer Coaches Needed

Our recreational program depends on parent volunteers to form teams. Most of our coaches start with little or no soccer experience—the league can provide drills, practice ideas, and support to help you get started.

Tip: If two parents volunteer as co-coaches, responsibilities can be shared.

Teams cannot be formed without volunteer coaches. If we do not have enough coaches, some players may be placed on a waiting list.

More information (coaching roles & responsibilities)

Head Coach Responsibilities

  • Parent communications about schedule changes, uniforms, and team updates
  • Organizing and running practices
  • Contacting the opposing coach prior to the game to confirm details
  • Providing payment to the referee for home games
  • Entering game scores online

Assistant Coach Responsibilities

  • Supporting the head coach during practices and games
  • Fulfilling head coach duties when the head coach is not present
  • Helping keep players on task and engaged

Schedule Details

  • Games: Saturdays within Lancaster County (usually mornings, some afternoons)
  • U8 / U6 Games: Home games on Thursdays during normal practice time
  • End-of-season: Tournament at Solanco
